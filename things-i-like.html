what is the meaning of life?

there are many interpretations of what life is and can be. there are people who believe we are here because a divine being created us, the landscape, oceans, other living creatures, and the entire universe. others believe that we evolved from microbes millions of years ago and were cursed with consciousness. there also those of us believe that life is pointless and that when the time comes for us to pass, there is nothing waiting for us. in my opinion, i believe that we should not be too caught up on what life could be or is not. i stand with the belief that life is what you make it, and i will continue to live life that way because there is no other statement truer than that. life was created from a higher being. life is from microbes that spawned millions of years ago. life is pointless! life is everything all at once, isn't it wonderful?
