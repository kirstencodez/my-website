WHAT MAKES ME HAPPY?

my friends!

growing up in a military family meant i never got to stay anywhere for too long, so friends were always coming and going. i never realized how important it was to maintain friendships until i met my best friend, keeley. we've travelled to japan and korea together, and have been friends since the eighth grade.

concerts !

man, when am i NOT going to a concert? this year i've been to three so far, but from this point until the end of the year, i have five more concerts to go to! i love all kinds of music, but kpop really caught me by surprise. the three concerts i went to this year were all kpop concerts.

travel

travelling!

as previously mentioned, i grew up in a military family so i'm used to moving around all the time. i love leaving bremerton and seeing what other parts of the world i have yet to touch, or have been wanting to touch again. last year i went to several countries in europe, japan, and korea! i like being able to escape my responsibilities for a little while and trying new foods, meeting new people, and immersing myself in a different culture.
